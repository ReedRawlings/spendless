## Introduction

SpendLess ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SpendLess mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use SpendLess, you may provide us with the following types of information:

- **Account Information:** Email address (optional, for email subscriptions and lead magnets)
- **Goal Information:** Financial goals, target savings amounts, goal names, and target dates
- **Waiting List Items:** Product names, prices, and links for items you're considering purchasing
- **Personal Preferences:** Intervention settings, notification preferences, and app configuration
- **Optional Profile Data:** Birth year (for life energy calculations), hourly wage (for opportunity cost features)

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the App, including features used, buttons tapped, and time spent in the App
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Screen Time Data:** When you access blocked apps (processed entirely on-device)

### Screen Time API Usage

SpendLess uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls framework) to:
- Block access to shopping apps you choose to restrict
- Display shield screens when you attempt to open blocked apps
- Track when blocked apps are accessed (for streak and analytics purposes)

**Important:** All Screen Time data is processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to information about which specific apps you block or when you use them on our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide App Functionality:** Enable goal tracking, waiting list management, and app blocking features
- **Personalize Your Experience:** Customize interventions, calculate life energy costs, and display relevant content
- **Send Notifications:** Deliver waiting list reminders, streak celebrations, and goal progress updates (with your permission)
- **Process Subscriptions:** Manage your subscription status and provide access to premium features
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features
- **Communicate with You:** Send email updates if you've opted into our mailing list

## Data Storage and Security

### On-Device Storage

The majority of your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:
- Goals and savings progress
- Waiting list items
- Graveyard (buried items)
- Streak data
- User preferences and settings

This data is stored in your device's secure container and is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

### Cloud Services

We use the following third-party services:

#### Apple App Store (Subscription Management)
- **Purpose:** Process and manage subscriptions via native StoreKit
- **Data Shared:** Subscription transactions processed through Apple's secure payment system
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)

#### MailerLite (Email Marketing) - Optional
- **Purpose:** Send email updates and downloadable resources to users who opt-in
- **Data Shared:** Email address, marketing preferences
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control Your Data

You can:
- **View Your Data:** Access all your goals, waiting list items, and settings within the App
- **Delete Your Data:** Delete individual items or reset all app data through Settings
- **Export Your Data:** [Contact us to request a data export]

If you've subscribed to our email list, you can:

To delete all data associated with your use of SpendLess:
1. Open Settings in the App
2. Select "Reset All Data"
